About me

Due to that, I started to complete my business background with digital and tech skills, either by self-learning or by attending to courses in the fields of digital marketing and programming.

So, I started a few startups and after analyzing all the problems I faced and what I did wrong, I founded Startup Center, a non-for-profit organization aimed to encourage entrepreneur spirit between young people in Spain and to help them to launch and accelerate their projects.

Always looking forward to taking part of amazing projects.Aimed to become a better professional and learn new things each day.
